**What is an Instruction?**
An instruction is the directive text that specifies the task a student must perform. It differs from the root text (the main content/context of the question) by providing the actionable command. Instructions typically appear as the final sentence(s) before answer options begin and may exist independently without root text.

**Definition of Explicit Instructions**:
Explicit instructions clearly specify the exact number of correct answers expected from the student.

**Scope**:
- Applied to multiple-choice and true-false question types
- Single-choice questions are always explicit by nature
- Mapping questions are always implicit by nature

**Identification Criteria**:
An instruction is explicit when it DOES specify the exact number of correct answers.

Examples:
(single-choice) Entscheide, ob die Aussagen falsch oder richtig sind.
(single-choice) Wähle die richtige Antwortmöglichkeit aus!
(multiple-choice) Wähle die beiden korrekten Aussagen aus.
(multiple-choice) Wähle die zwei richtigen Antworten aus!
(true-false) Falsch oder richtig? Wähle die zwei richtigen Antworten aus!

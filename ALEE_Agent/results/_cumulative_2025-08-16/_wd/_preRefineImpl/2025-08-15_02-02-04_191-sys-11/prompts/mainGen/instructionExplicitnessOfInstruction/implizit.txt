**What is an Instruction?**
An instruction is the directive text that specifies the task a student must perform. It differs from the root text (the main content/context of the question) by providing the actionable command. Instructions typically appear as the final sentence(s) before answer options begin and may exist independently without root text.

**Definition of Implicit Instructions**: 
Implicit instructions do not specify the exact number of correct answers expected from the student.

**Scope**: 
- Applied to multiple-choice and true-false question types
- Mapping questions are always implicit by nature
- Single-choice questions are always explicit by nature

**Identification Criteria**:
An instruction is implicit when it does NOT specify the exact number of correct answers.
Examples:
(multiple-choice) Wähle die zutreffenden Aussagen aus. Es können auch mehrere Antworten richtig sein.
(multiple-choice) Wähle die Antworten aus, in welchen die Grenzen des Modellautos deutlich werden.
(true-false) Entscheide, ob die Aussagen falsch oder richtig sind.
(true-false) Kreuze die zutreffenden Aussagen an.
(mapping) Ordne die links stehenden Beschreibung den richtigen Begriffen zu.
(mapping) Ordne die Begriffe richtig zu.
